About William Alexander Ivanov

 Hi, I'm Will and I'm a 15 year old sophomore who goes to Summit Rainier Public School. I was born in Fairfax County Virginia. I lived in Washington DC for most of my early childhood, except for fouth grade, when I went to live with my family in Romania for a year. There, I lived with my grandparents, aunt, and cousins. The next year, I came back to the U.S. In 5th grade, my dad got a job offer in California, so we moved to San Jose. In 8th grade, I went to Romania for another year. I learned a lot of math so when I came back in 9th grade, I was placed in math II.

Traveling

 I have been to over 16 countries. I visit my family in Romania almost every summer, and we often visit other nearby countries. For 1 night, I stayed at a hotel in Istanbul, by myself. I was flying back to the U.S. after visiting Romania, and had 2 flights: one from Bucharest, Romania to Istanbul, Turkey, and another from Istanbul home, in San Jose. My first flight left 20 minutes late, and I only had 1 hour inbetween flights. By the time I got through security, my other flight left without me. The airline I was flying with gave me a new flight the next day (for free), and payed for food, transportation, and a hotel. I left the next day, and got home that night.
